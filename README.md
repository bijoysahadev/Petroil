# Petroil - Oil & Gas Industry Landing Page

This project is a high-performance, professional website designed for a major national energy supplier. It showcases the company's long-standing history and specialized services in modern natural oil and gas refineries.

### Key Features:
* **Industry Focused Design:** A clean, industrial aesthetic tailored for the energy sector.
* **Service Highlights:** Dedicated sections for natural oil and gas refinery services.
* **Fully Responsive:** Optimized for viewing across various devices.
* **Modern Layout:** Includes sections for industry blogging, company certifications, and contact information.

### Technologies Used:
* HTML5 / CSS3
* FontAwesome icons
* Google Fonts (Poppins)
